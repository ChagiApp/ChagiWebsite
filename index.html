Why Chagi?

We all know that it takes a lot of time and effort to be a great martial artist. In an ideal world, we would train with our instructors 7 days a week, but life gets in the way.

Practice when it suits you

Sometimes we've got to drop the kids off at football practice or  it's the dog's birthday. Whatever the reason, there are times we can't get to training. With this you will be able to support your practice when you can make it to the dojang.

Support your work in the dojang

Time in the dojang is important but so is doing your homework and supporting your practice. Extra time practicing will always make you better at TKD.

What is Chagi?

Chagi is an offline app that is being developed to help TKD players practice away from their dojang.

Is it good for me?

Chagi is useful to anyone that wants a way to help organise their exercises away from the dojang.
